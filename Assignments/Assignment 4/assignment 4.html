Suits: A Brief Overview

Suits is a legal drama that premiered on USA Network in 2011. It follows Mike Ross, a college dropout who begins working as a law associate despite never attending law school. The show ran for 9 seasons and became a fan favorite.

Basic Statistics

  • Number of Seasons: 9
  • Total Episodes: 134
  • Average Viewership per Episode: 2.97 million
  • Maximum Viewership: 4.64 million
  • Minimum Viewership: 1.3 million

Summary of Observations

  • The highest viewership occurred in the early seasons, peaking at around 4.64 million viewers.
  • Between Season 3 and Season 5, viewership dropped by approximately 0.7 million viewers.
  • The last seasons maintained moderate but stable audience levels with an average of around 1.53 million viewers.
